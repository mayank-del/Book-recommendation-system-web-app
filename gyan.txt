There are four type of recommendation system
(1) A popularity-based recommendation system is one in which recommendations are made based on a formula or the content that is most frequently visited.
(2) Content based is one in which recommendations are made based on columns in the dataset like we can recommend all movies of same hero,same production houses movies same director etc.
(3) Collaborative filter System is on in which recommendation is based on content that is positively reviewed by users.
(4) Hybrid recommendation system is that in which we merge concept of all three system and give weightage to all thses three according to our requirements.
